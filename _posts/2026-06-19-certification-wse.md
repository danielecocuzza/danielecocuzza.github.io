# I Have Reached a C1 Level in English

I am happy to share a new achievement in my personal and professional journey: I have obtained a **CEFR C1 English certificate** from **Wall Street English School**.

I attended the course from **July 16, 2025**, to **June 19, 2026**, with the goal of improving my language skills and becoming more confident in using English in academic, professional, and everyday contexts.

For me, this result represents an important step, especially because English plays a major role in **Artificial Intelligence**, **scientific research**, and **software development**. Most technical documentation, research papers, and international collaborations require a strong command of the language.

This certificate is not a final destination, but a confirmation of the work I have done and a motivation to keep improving. My goal is to use English more and more, both in my studies and in professional communication.
