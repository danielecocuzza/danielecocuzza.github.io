I was awarded a research grant at the **University of Catania** titled **AI-Surveillance: Artificial Intelligence for Urban Security and Monitoring.**
The research activity is carried out within the **IP Lab** research group at the **Department of Mathematics and Computer Science**, **University of Catania.**
The project focuses on the application of **Artificial Intelligence** and **Computer Vision** techniques to support urban safety, monitoring, and surveillance systems.
